# Advosy Company Context

## Quick Reference

**What is Advosy?** A home services parent company in Arizona using EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System).

**Core Principles:** Versatility, Growth, Win

**Subsidiaries:**
- **Select Adjusters** - Public Adjusting (PA)
- **VRZA** - Roofing Construction (new branding)
  - **Image Roofing** - Specialty roofs (under VRZA)
- **Advosy Construction** - Roofing (old branding, being replaced)
- **Everest Air** - HVAC and Plumbing
- **Bloque Restoration** - Restoration and Mitigation
- **Pestkee** - Pest Control (just launched)
- **Southwest Packout** - Packout Services

**Related but separate:** Panda Promo (Gary Fenn's side business)
